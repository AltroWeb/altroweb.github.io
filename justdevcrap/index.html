About JustDevCrap

Welcome to the digital dumpster fire that is JustDevCrap's portfolio. I'm a developer who believes in pushing the boundaries of what's possible... and occasionally, what's stable. My code is like a box of chocolates: you never know what you're gonna get (mostly bugs, though).

I specialize in turning caffeine into code, and sometimes, that code even works. My passion lies in creating software that's both innovative and slightly terrifying. If you're looking for perfection, you've come to the wrong place. If you're looking for someone who gets things done, even if it involves a bit of duct tape and prayer, then you're in for a treat.

Join me on this chaotic journey through the world of development, where every commit is an adventure and every deployment is a gamble.
