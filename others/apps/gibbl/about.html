Architecture

The Single-File Micro-Platform

Most social networks require hundreds of thousands of files and heavy Node.js backends. gibbl. is completely housed within a single HTML file. The rendering, security protocols, routing, and UI state are all managed locally using ultra-optimized Vanilla JS and DOM diffing.

Backend

Serverless & Secure

Powered by Firebase Firestore & Authentication. Your data connects directly and securely from your device to the cloud. We utilize rigorous Firestore Security Rules and Intrusion Detection engines right on the client side to keep your account safe.

FAQ

Is my data secure?
Absolutely. All passwords are encrypted by Google's Firebase Auth. Our database rules are strictly locked down—only your authorized devices can edit your profile.
Is there an algorithm?
No. The feed is strictly chronological, and the "Top" feed is purely based on likes. We don't track your behavior to serve you addictive content.
Is it open source?
The concept and frontend architecture are completely open by nature (you can literally inspect the page source to see the entire app engine).
