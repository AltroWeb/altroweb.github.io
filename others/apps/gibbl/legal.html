Privacy Policy

Your data is yours. We collect the absolute minimum required to make the app function.

What We Collect

  • Account Basics: Your email address, display name, handle, and a hashed version of your password.
  • Content: The posts, replies, and compressed media (images/audio/video up to 800KB) you actively publish.
  • Device Security Data: Basic User-Agent strings and active session timestamps to power our Intrusion Detection engine (allowing you to sign out rogue devices).

Third-Party Infrastructure

gibbl is powered by Google's infrastructure. We do not sell your data to advertisers, but we use the following services to keep the lights on:

  • Firebase Auth & Firestore: Used to securely store your account data and host the database. Passwords are encrypted by Google.
  • Google reCAPTCHA v3: Runs invisibly in the background during login and signup to block bots and spam. Their Privacy Policy applies to this security check.

Your Right to Vanish

We built a "Nuclear Option" into the settings. Clicking "Delete Account" executes a batch script that instantly wipes your profile, posts, notifications, and traces of your account from our Firestore database. No 30-day waiting periods. Once it's gone, it's gone.

Terms of Service

By using gibbl, you agree to play by the rules of the vibe.

1. The "Unbiased" Rule

gibbl is an algorithm-free zone. We do not curate, shadowban, or artificially boost content based on political bias, corporate sponsorships, or "engagement optimization."

  • The Newest feed is strictly chronological.
  • The Top feed is strictly based on integer math (like counts).

Everyone gets the exact same chronological megaphone.

2. Free Expression & Boundaries

We support open dialogue and free expression. However, "free speech" is not a free pass to break the law or destroy the platform. You will be permanently banned for posting:

  • Illegal content, CSAM, or explicit non-consensual media.
  • Direct, credible threats of violence or doxxing.
  • Spam, bot-nets, or attempts to crash the 1MB database document limits (don't paste movie scripts).

3. User Responsibility

You own what you post. gibbl is a neutral platform. We are not legally responsible for the opinions, media, or external links posted by our users. If you upload media, you verify that you have the right to share it.

4. Service Limits

gibbl is an engineering experiment built to be blazing fast. We reserve the right to enforce capacity caps (e.g., maximum 500 users, 800KB media limits) to ensure the platform remains stable and free for the community.
